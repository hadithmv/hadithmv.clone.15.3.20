Frequently Asked Questions

For questions that are not listed here please reach out and contact us.



What is the slogan of hadithmv?

The Maldivian Hadith translation platform.


What is hadith?

They are the sayings, actions, approvals as well as the physical characteristics and manners of Muhammad ﷺ, the last Prophet sent by Allah. Hadith is also called Sunnah. After the Quran, hadith is the second source of all sharia legislations as well as behavioural etiquette standards in Islam. We can only know what we should and should not do through the Messenger ﷺ. Following the Messenger ﷺ is the only way to salvation and all that is good, and choosing any other path is falling into wretchedness and destruction, in this world and the next.


Is hadithmv on social media?

Yes, hadithmv can be found on Facebook, Viber, Telegram, Instagram and Twitter. See the very bottom of this page for links.


What is Takhrij?

Takhreej is referencing the hadith text to the source book from which it is originally found.


Is there a dark mode / night theme?

The site automatically switches the theme based on your system preferences, however the app does not as of yet.


What is hadithmv, and what is it for?

Please read about that in detail here.


How is hadithmv different?

It is the first program of its kind in the Dhivehi language, and as far as we are aware, the only.


Where is hadithmv based?

In the Maldives, although most of the code and content was written in Madina.


Who is hadithmv for?

Quite simply, everyone. From students to teachers, children to parents, and seekers of knowledge to scholars, etc.


What can I do on hadithmv?

You can read translations of Quran, Hadith, Aqida and Duas. You can use hadithmv like a dictionary. You can find the source references for these books. You can lookup texts in Dhivehi as well as Arabic, and you can search even without Arabic diacritics. You can copy and paste the translations from and to any device, through any kind of media. You can select how many items to display on the screen at one time. You can jump to any page. You can use filter options to see only specific texts. You can show and hide different types of text. You can use keyboard shortcuts on the desktop.


I learned about a hadith, can I issue fatwa or answer fiqh questions?

No, that is only something the scholars can do. The Sharia of Allah is not spoken of except with knowledge. While hadithmv is intended to be a resource for various Islamic texts, one or two hadith alone is not sufficient for layman to have formed a comprehensive opinion on a matter. Many individuals who haven not studied even the basics of Islam or know even the slightest Arabic think that because they have the internet, they are as capable as any scholar. This is nothing but a fallacy. You cannot come up with your own fiqh nor make up your own analogy when it comes to Islam. Anything related to fiqh or fatwa should always be directed to the real scholars.


Are all the hadith here authentic?

If a hadith reference is from Bukhari or Muslim, then it is undoubtedly authentic. For any other book, the grading of a hadith scholar is needed before saying it is authentic. Some references do include such gradings, while others do not have them yet.


What numbering system do you use to reference hadith?

We use the indexing scheme of Muhammad Fuad Abdul Baaqi wherever possible, as it is the most used and easiest to look up. Otherwise we use the numbering order found in the Shamela library program.

What is your Aqidah?

My Aqida is that of Ahlu Sunnah wal Jama'ah. I follow the Quran and Sunnah upon the understanding of the Companions. I affirm the Names and Attributes of Allah as Allah and his Messenger ﷺ did, without changing their wording, interpreting them as metaphorical, denying them, likening them to the creation, or asking how they are. I hear and obey even if a slave is over me and I give advice to those in authority privately and not on the streets. This is what was taught in the Islamic University of Madina.


How do you make content so fast?

It is mainly due to modernized workflow and tools, we tend to automate things as much as possible. This allows us to create a weeks worth of content that otherwise would have taken months had we done everything manually.

Why is some content missing from a book?

Most likely because it is still in the process of being written and checked, it will be fully available once ready.


What will happen to hadithmv if something happens to you?

That has been discussed within the team, and we have protocols in place so that the project may continue with or without me. It has already been decided who takes over and directs hadithmv, so no worries about the work stopping in sha Allah.


Why is the site different from the app?

The site gets updated first, so the latest changes will always be available there. We only roll updates to the app a while after.


Can I join the hadithmv project?

We are looking for people who have skills in on or all of these three: Dhivehi, Arabic, and programming. Roles include translating, proof-reading and coding. Contact us for more information if you want to join.


What are you looking for in volunteers?

Applicants should be self-motivated, responsive, able to meet deadlines, and not feel offended when corrected.



How else can I help hadithmv?

You can also help by rating the app, note that you can rate without showing your name if you don't write anything. Another way to assist is by sharing hadithmv with your friends. If you ever wanted to spread Islamic knowledge in Dhivehi, or maybe even thought about distributing a great many books to Maldivians all around the world so that they can benefit, then sharing hadithmv is as easy as it gets.


Is hadithmv free?

Of course. It has and always will be free, in sha Allah.


Which books are currently available?

You can find those on the main page. We might have some other books ready, but awaiting further checks before being shared publicly. The content we have drafted and in-waiting is actually more than the content we show on the site.


Which books will be available soon?

We focus mainly on hadith. But we also include content on Quran, Aqida, Duas and language when we feel it appropriate. So what we are currently working on will constantly be changing, consider joining the translation team to be in the loop.


How do you source your arabic text?

We usually rely upon the texts of the Shamela library program, unless specified otherwise. We look for the most accessible source for the text, as that is what most people will find easily available, then we use the best references we can find even if that is only in a hardcover book.


I would like to know more about a particular book?

Check if there's a link to an about page where that book is found. Otherwise contact us for more information.


Are the translations correct?

Yes, in sha Allah. We do our best to make them as accurate and understandable as possible. Please report any inaccuracies so that we may correct them as soon as possible.


Are the translations approved by the Islamic Ministry?

The books approved by the Islamic Ministry will have an approval reference number on its about page. Others maybe in the making or pending approval.


Who made and maintains hadithmv?

(Abu Yahya) Mohamed Ashraaf Ibrahim. Student of the Hadith Faculty, Islamic University of Madina.


Why do you call yourself Yahyas Dad / Abu Yahya?

Because that is my Kunyah, which is a kind of arabic nickname where a person is called after their oldest child.


Who else participates in the hadithmv team?

hadithmv has had various helpers over time. Most of them are friends and colleagues from the Islamic University of Madina, as well as people who are skilled in the Dhivehi and Arabic language, as well as those who help with programming. For a list of contributors please click here.


When was hadithmv started?

Jul 17, 2017.


Is there an app?

Yes. While hadithmv is primarily available online on the web, it is also available offline as an Android and Windows app.


Is there an iOS app?

Not yet. However, iOS users can get the same functionality as the android app from the mobile web version of hadithmv. In sha Allah there will be an iOS app, contact us if you would like to help with that.


How do I continue from the last place I was at a later time?

You can use the web version of hadithmv, the page will stay as is even if you close the browser. Alternatively you can save the URL.


Why not make seperate apps for all of the books?

The purpose of hadithmv is to have everything accessible in one place. A centralized, unified approach is the most efficient in terms of size, speed and development. In short, to avoid redundancy and bloat. Otherwise a hundred books would be a hundred apps on each seperate platform.


Why is it called hadithmv? and why are there things other than hadith on it?

It started out as a website providing the Dhivehi translation of Nawawi's 40 hadith, then later developed into much more than that. While the main focus will always be on reviving the Sunnah and making it accessible for everyone, it became important to include everything that helps in achieving that goal. The most important of which would be the Quran without a doubt, followed by books on the correct Aqida. Supporting language material for learning Arabic and translating was also found to be indispensable.

ޙަދީޘްއެމްވީ ފެށުނީ ކޮން އިރަކު؟

ޖުލައި 17، 2017.


އެޕްލިކޭޝަން އެއް ހުރޭތަ؟

އާދެ. އޮންލައިންކޮށް ޙަދީޘްއެމްވީ ވެބްގައި ބޭނުން ކުރެވޭނެ. އަދި އޮފްލައިންކޮށް އޭންޑްރޮއިޑް އަދި ވިންޑޯސް އެޕްލިކޭޝަން ބޭނުން ކުރެވޭނެ.


އައިއޯއެސް ވަރޝަން އެއް ހުރޭތަ؟

އަދި ނެތް. އެކަމަކު ޙަދީޘްއެމްވީގެ ވެބް ވަރޝަން، ހަމަ އެންޑްރޮއިޑް އެޕްއާ އެއްފަދައިން އައިއޯއެސް ބޭނުން ކުރާ ފަރާތްތަކަށް ބޭނުން ކުރެވޭނެ.


ކިޔަން ހުރި ތަން ފަހުން ހޯދާނީ ކިހިނެތް؟

ޙަދީޘްއެމްވީގެ ވެބް ވަރޝަން ބޭނުން ކުރެވިދާނެ، އޭރުން ބްރައުސަރ ލެއްޕިއަސް ކިޔަން ހުރި ތަނެއް ނުގެއްލޭނެ. އަނެއް ގޮތަކީ ޔޫއާރެލް ސޭވް ކުރުން.


ކޮންމެ ފޮތަކަށް ވަކި އެޕްލިކޭޝަނެއް ނަހަދަނީ ކީއްވެ؟

ޙަދީޘްއެމްވީގެ މަގްސަދަކީ ހުރިހާ އެއްޗެއް އެއްތާކުން ފަސޭހައިން ލިބޭނެ ގޮތް ހެދުމެވެ. އެއްތާކު، އެކަތިގަނޑެއްގެ ގޮތުގައި ހެދުމަކީ ސައިޒާއި، ހަލުވިކަމާއި، ތަރައްގީ ކުރުމަށް ބަލާއިރު އެންމެ ރަނގަޅު ގޮތެވެ. ކުރު ގޮތަކަށް ބުނާނަމަ، ތަކުރާރުކަމާއި ބަރުވުމުން ދުރުހެލިވުމަށް ޓަކައެވެ. ނޫން ނަމަ، ކޮންމެ މަންސައެއްގައި ސަތޭކަ ފޮތް ވާނީ ސަތޭކަ އެޕްލިކޭޝަނަށެވެ.


ޙަދީޘްއެމްވީ އޭ ކިޔުނީ ކީއްވެ؟ އަދި ޙަދީޘް ނޫން އެއްޗެހި ހުންނަނީ ކީއްވެ؟

ޙަދީޘްއެމްވީ ފެށިގެން އައީ ނަވަވީގެ 40 ޙަދީޘްގެ ދިވެހި ތަރުޖަމާ ހިއްސާ ކުރުމަށް ހެދުނު ވެބްސައިޓެއް ގޮތަށެވެ. އޭގެ ފަހުން ވަރަށް ބޮޑަށް ތަރައްގީ ކުރެވި ބޮޑެތި ބަދަލުތަކެއް ގެނެވުނެވެ. އެހެނަސް މަައިގަނޑު އަމާޒަކަށް ސުންނަތް ދިރުވުމައި ފޯރުކޮށްދިނުން އަބަދުވެސް ވާނެއެވެ. ހަމައެއާއެކު މި ލަނޑުދަނޑިއާ ހަމައަށް ވާސިލުކޮށްދޭ އެންމެހައި ކަންތައްތައް ހިމެނުންވެސް މުހިންމުވެގެން ދިޔައެވެ. އޭގެ ތެރެއިން އެންމެ މުހިންމު ވާނީ ހަމަ ޔަގީނުންވެސް ކީރިތި ޤުރްއާނެވެ. އެއަށްފަހު ސައްހަ އަގީދާގެ ފޮތްތަކެވެ. ހަމައެފަދައިން އަރަބި ބަސް ދަސްކުރުމާއި ތަރުޖަމާކުރުމުގެ މައުލޫމާތު ހުރި ފޮތްތަކުގެ ބޭނުންތެރިކަން ނުހަނު ބޮޑެވެ.
