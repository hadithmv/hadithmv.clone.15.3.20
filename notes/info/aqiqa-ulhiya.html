For those who want to offer their sacrifice or build a well abroad.



Aqiqa and Ulhiya

  • The animals and their sacrifice will be according to Islamic and medical guidelines, in sha Allah.
  • The name of on whose behalf it is slaughtered will be printed out and placed on the animal.
  • Photos & videos of the slaughter will be sent to the client as proof, the meat will be distributed to the poor & needy.
  • Orders need to be placed at least 1 working day in advance of the sacrifice, to order please send a message on either Telegram, Facebook or Email.

  • ޖަނަވާރު ކަތިލެވޭ ފަރާތުގެ ނަން ގަނޑަކަށް ޕްރިންޓު ކުރެވި ޖަނަވާރުގައި ތަތް ކުރެވޭނެ.
  • ޖަނަވާރު ކަތިލުމުގެ ފޮޓޯއާއި ވީޑިއޯ ހެކީގެ ގޮތުގައި ވެރިފަރާތަށް ފޮނުވޭނެ، އަދި ކަތިލެވޭ ޖަނަވާރުގެ މަސް ފަޤީރުންނާއި މިސްކީނުންނަށް ބެހޭނެ.

Dig a waterwell

  • We will look for needy muslim families to build the well for, who are usually around 5 - 10 or more members.
  • Tube wells will be dug by hand tools, fitted with pipe casing and a hand-pump, with cement splash slab. The names of the donors will be fixed on the well plate.
  • Photos & videos of the the well during construction and after will be sent to the client as proof
  • Wells can usually be built within 2 weeks, depending on schedule. To order please send a message on either Telegram, Facebook or Email.
