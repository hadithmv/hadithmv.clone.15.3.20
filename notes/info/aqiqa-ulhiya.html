hadithmv - Aqiqa and Ulhiya

For those who want to offer their sacrifice abroad for an affordable price.


  • Only animals deemed Islamically acceptable and medically fit shall be selected for sacrifice.
  • The name of the one on whose behalf it is slaughtered will be printed out and placed on the animal.
  • Photos and videos of the animal and its slaughter will be sent to the client as proof.
  • All animals will be sacrificed in a Sharia compliant manner according to proper Islamic etiquette.
  • The meat from the sacrificed animal will be distributed to the poor and needy.
  • Orders need to be placed in advance. (atleast 3 days prior for Chad, 1 day for Nepal and Egypt.)
  • To place orders, send a message on either Telegram, Facebook or Email.
