For those who want to offer their sacrifice abroad for an affordable price

  • Only animals deemed Islamically and medically fit shall be selected.
  • The name of the one on whose behalf it is slaughtered will be printed out and placed on the animal.
  • Photos and videos of the animal and its slaughter will be sent to the client as proof.
  • All animals will be sacrificed in a Sharia compliant manner according to proper Islamic etiquette.
  • Orders need to be placed atleast 2 days prior to the sacrifice. (1 day prior for Nepal orders)
  • For urgent cases, sacrifice can be arranged within the same day of the order for 965/- MVR (Nepal goat), provided you make the order before 11am (Maldives time).
  • To place orders, contact us either on Telegram, Facebook or Email.
