Note that when you send typos to us, don’t just send screenshots, rather also copy the plain text as is and send that. This makes it easier for us to find those errors and fix them sooner.

In case the mistake you found has already been corrected, check whether it is still there on the online Hadithmv website. If it looks okay there, then there’s nothing for us to fix, and it is the responsibility of third party developers of other apps to update their data from Hadithmv.

In case your question/suggestion has already been addressed, click here to read the FAQ first.
