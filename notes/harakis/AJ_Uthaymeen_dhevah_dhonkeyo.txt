• murααsil

Adam Nishan, Jihaadhuge mathiverikamaai waajibukamaai farudhu ayneh kamaai, eyah inkaaru kuraameehaa murutadhdhu vaaney kamah ethakeh dhaleeleh genaeve. Dhen nimmaalamun Utheymeen ge baheh genes "ilmu haasilu kurun" eyah vure mathiveri kamaai muhimummu kamah bunyeve.

Dhen Ali Jaleel رحمه الله bunyeve:
"e hurihaa varugadha dhaleel thakah fahu, Utheymeen ge dheVah dhonkeyo eynaa beyvvi eve" 

Ali Jaleel رحمه الله
