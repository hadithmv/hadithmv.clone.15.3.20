The official Hadithmv app for Android.

This is a collection of around four thousand hadith in the Maldivian language, from books such as:
• 40 Nawawi
• Umdatul Ahkam
• Sahih Bukhari
• Sahih Muslim
as well as many others.
May Allah aid me in adding more. Ameen.

Features of the Hadithmv app include:

• Unique:
First Dhivehi hadith app ever developed.

• Sourced:
Reference numbers match the hadith books.

• Searchable:
Easily look up hadith in both Arabic and Dhivehi.

• Diacritic-agnostic:
Supports Arabic search with and without tashkheel.

• Accessible:
Find, copy and share these hadith anywhere.

• Simple:
Clean and easy to use minimalistic interface.

• Lightweight:
Small app size and efficient code that loads up instantly.

• Cross-platform:
Available on the Web, Android and Windows 10.

• 100% free:
Fully featured, only a phone or computer required.

• Offline:
Works perfectly without an internet connection.
